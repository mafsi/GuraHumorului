Welcome

A living gallery of faith, colour, and craft

Since the 15th century, the monasteries around Gura Humorului have carried their frescoes through winters and centuries alike. Today the town is the quiet gateway to that world — and, most evenings, the market square still smells of woodsmoke and mulled wine.

Winter carols in the old market
