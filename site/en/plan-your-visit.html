Plan Your Visit

Build your route through Bucovina

Two hours from Suceava by car, or one train from Bucharest — most visitors spend two to four unhurried days between the painted monasteries, the old town, and the hills around it.

What You Can Do

Three ways to spend your days here

Every visit blends the same three threads — pick the balance that suits you, then see the full calendar of dates on the Experiences page.

Festivals & Traditions

Church-calendar gatherings and market days — colinde in December, painted eggs at Holy Week, the wool and harvest fair in September.

See dates & details

Outdoor & Trails

Waymarked hikes and rides through the Obcinele Bucovinei, plus a gentler spring run down the Moldova river.

See dates & details

Food & Craft

Slow suppers, hand looms, and Saturday market tastings — the valley's quieter, everyday rhythm.

See dates & details
Best Time to Come

Three seasons, three reasons

April brings the painted egg fair before Easter, September the wool and harvest gathering, December the carol-singing season — everything else runs quietly, on its own schedule, and rewards travellers who prefer fewer crowds.
