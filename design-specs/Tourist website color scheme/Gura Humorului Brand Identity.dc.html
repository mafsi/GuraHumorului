Brand Identity Guide

Gura Humorului

Bucovina · Romania — Heritage Tourism
01 — Brand Essence

An old-money quiet confidence — the restraint of a place that doesn't need to shout about what it has kept for six hundred years.

Tone
Warm, unhurried, precise. Never exclamatory.
Audience
Cultural travellers who value heritage, craft, and quiet landscapes over spectacle.
Not this
No emoji, no bright primary colours, no crowded layouts.
02 — Logo

A wordmark-led identity — classic serif name with a tracked tagline, no separate mark. Consistency in the name's setting carries the brand.

Clear space & minimum size

Keep clear space equal to the cap-height of "G" on every side. Never set the wordmark below 18px cap-height — at that scale, drop the tagline.

Don't

Don't italicise, condense, recolour outside the palette, add a drop shadow, or place on a busy photograph without a scrim.
