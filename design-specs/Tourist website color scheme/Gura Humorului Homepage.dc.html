Where painted monasteries meet ancient forest

Welcome

A living gallery of faith, colour, and craft

Since the 15th century, the monasteries around Gura Humorului have carried their frescoes through winters and centuries alike. Today the town is the quiet gateway to that world — trailheads, workshops, and guesthouses within a short walk of the old market square.
